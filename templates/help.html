Search Help & Syntax Guide

Basic Search

Enter keywords to find documents containing those terms:

machine learning

Finds documents containing either "machine" OR "learning"

Boolean Operators

Combine search terms with Boolean operators:

AND

neural AND networks

Finds documents containing both "neural" AND "networks"

OR

classification OR clustering

Finds documents containing either "classification" OR "clustering"

NOT

machine learning NOT supervised

Finds documents containing "machine learning" BUT NOT "supervised"

Combining Operators

neural AND networks NOT convolutional

Finds documents containing both "neural" AND "networks" BUT NOT "convolutional"

Field Search

Search within specific fields using the field:term syntax:

author:smith

Finds documents where the author field contains "smith"

title:learning

Finds documents where the title contains "learning"

author:smith AND title:neural

Finds documents by "smith" that also have "neural" in the title

Phrase Search

Search for exact phrases by enclosing them in quotation marks:

"machine learning"

Finds documents containing the exact phrase "machine learning"
