Our Mission

The Information Retrieval System is designed to provide researchers and academics with a powerful tool for searching scientific papers. Our mission is to make knowledge more accessible through advanced search capabilities and an intuitive user interface.

Key Features

  • Boolean search with AND, OR, and NOT operators
  • Spell correction for search queries
  • PDF document viewing
  • Advanced metadata display
  • Responsive design for all devices
